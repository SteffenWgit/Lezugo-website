Delete your Lezugo account

You can delete your Lezugo account directly inside the Lezugo app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Lezugo app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Enter your current password.
  6. Type DELETE to confirm.
  7. Confirm the deletion.

After confirmation, your Lezugo account will be deleted and you will be signed out of the app.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your profile data, including your username
  • Your learning progress
  • Your vocabulary progress
  • Your review history
  • Your streak data
  • Your in-app rewards and apricot balance
  • Your collected reward cards
  • Your subscription records stored in Lezugo

Data that may be retained

Some technical records may be retained for a limited period where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup recovery. These records are not used to restore your account.

Important

Account deletion is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your learning progress and app data cannot be restored.

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting support at: support@lezugo-app.com
